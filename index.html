The Area

Why people drive
200 miles into the desert

Marfa defies description. It's a tiny West Texas ranch town that somehow became one of the most quietly electrifying places in America — equal parts art world and cowboy country, with skies that make you realize how small you've been living.

Walk to the Chinati Foundation. Drive out to Prada Marfa. Have a drink at the hotel bar. Watch a thunderstorm roll across the Chihuahuan Desert from your picnic table. Stargaze. Do nothing, beautifully.

308 S. Russell puts you right in the center of it — and far enough from it all to remember why you came.
